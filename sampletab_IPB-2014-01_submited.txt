Submission Title	Metabolic and transcriptional response of Arabidopsis thaliana wildtype and mutants to Phytophtora infestans	
Submission Identifier	GSB-68	
Submission Description	The oomycete P. infestans is the causal agent of late blight, the most devastating potato disease. In contrast to potato, A. thaliana is able to successfully prevent colonization of the pathogen due to a multi-layered nonhost resistance. Several mutants have been isolated which are impaired in penetration resistance. We used six different plant lines, the wildtype-like gl1, and the five different mutants (pen2, pen2erp1, pen2erp2, erp2D, erp140).
